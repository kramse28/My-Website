Sega Genesis

Information

History:

Launched in Japan in 1988 and North America in 1989, the Genesis was Sega’s major competitor to Nintendo during the 16-bit era.

Accomplishments:

  • Sold over 30 million units worldwide
  • Popularized faster-paced arcade-style action at home
  • Introduced Sonic the Hedgehog, becoming Sega’s flagship franchise
  • Helped establish aggressive console marketing strategies

Specifications:

  • Release Date: October 1988
  • Developer: Sega
  • Manufacturer: Sega
  • Generation: Fourth
  • Best-Selling Game: Sonic the Hedgehog
