Nintendo DS

Information

History:

Nintendo’s dual-screen handheld released in 2004, featuring a touchscreen and microphone.

Accomplishments:

  • Sold over 150 million units (one of the best-selling consoles ever)
  • Expanded gaming audience with titles like Nintendogs and Brain Age
  • Popularized touchscreen controls in gaming
  • Backward compatible with Game Boy Advance games

Specifications:

  • Release Date: November 2004

  • Developer: Nintendo

  • Manufacturer: Foxconn

  • Generation: Seventh

  • Best-Selling Game: New Super Mario Bros
