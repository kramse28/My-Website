Playstation One

Information

History:

Sony’s first home console, released in 1994 in Japan and 1995 worldwide, entering the market as a new competitor.

Accomplishments:

  • Sold over 100 million units (first console to do so)
  • Popularized CD-based gaming
  • Strong third-party support
  • Defined 3D gaming with titles like Final Fantasy VII

Specifications:

  • Release Date: December 1994
  • Developer: Sony Computer Entertainment
  • Manufacturer: Sony Electronics
  • Generation: Fifth
  • Best-Selling Game: Gran Turismo
