Dreamcast

Information

History:

Sega’s final home console, released in 1998 (Japan) and 1999 (North America).

Accomplishments:

  • First console with built-in modem for online play
  • Innovative VMU memory card with screen
  • Critically acclaimed games like Shenmue
  • Influenced future online console gaming despite modest sales (~9 million units)

Specifications:

  • Release Date: November 1998

  • Developer: Sega

  • Manufacturer: Sega

  • Generation: Sixth

  • Best-Selling Game: Sonic Adventure
