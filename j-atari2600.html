Atari 2600

Information

History:

Released in 1977 (originally as the Atari VCS), the Atari 2600 popularized interchangeable ROM cartridges, allowing players to switch games without buying a new system.

Accomplishments:

  • Helped create the modern home console market
  • Sold over 30 million units
  • Home to iconic titles like Space Invaders (first major arcade-to-home success)
  • Established third-party game development as a major industry force

Specifications:

  • Release Date: September 1977
  • Developer: Atari Inc.
  • Manufacturer: Atari Inc.
  • Generation: Second
  • Best-Selling Game: Pac-Man
